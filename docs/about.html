The Founders

Sebastian Hunt and Dylan Richards Diaz are the visionary duo behind entire studios. Their partnership began during their time working together at Yeezy, where they honed their skills in minimalist design and learned the importance of balancing form with function.

The Journey

From Tumblr to Yeezy

Before establishing entire studios, both founders cultivated their aesthetic sensibilities through digital platforms. Their early work on Tumblr showcased a keen eye for minimalist design and contemporary culture, which eventually led them to opportunities in high fashion.

The Yeezy Experience

Working under Kanye West's Yeezy brand was transformative for Hunt and Diaz. They gained invaluable experience in creating elevated basics, understanding fabric technology, and developing oversized silhouettes that have become synonymous with modern streetwear luxury.

Birth of entire studios

In 2020, armed with industry knowledge and a clear vision, they launched entire studios. Their goal was audacious yet simple: make luxury fashion accessible to everyone without compromising on quality or design integrity.

Design Philosophy

"We believe that great design should be democratic," says Hunt. "Our pieces are crafted with the same attention to detail you'd find in luxury houses, but we've restructured our business model to make them attainable."

The entire studios aesthetic is characterized by:

  • Clean Lines: Minimalist silhouettes that speak for themselves
  • Oversized Fits: Comfortable, contemporary proportions
  • Neutral Palettes: Timeless blacks, whites, and earth tones
  • Premium Materials: Carefully selected fabrics for durability and comfort
  • Functional Details: Thoughtful features that enhance wearability

Celebrity Following

Despite their "accessible luxury" approach, entire studios has garnered a cult following among A-list celebrities and fashion influencers:

  • Bad Bunny: Frequently spotted in the signature PFD Puffer
  • Kim Kardashian: Featured entire studios in multiple social posts
  • Lewis Hamilton: Wore the brand to various F1 events
  • Kylie Jenner: Collaborated on exclusive pieces
  • Travis Scott: Regular supporter of the brand

This organic celebrity endorsement proves that great design transcends price points—when given the choice, even those who can afford anything choose entire studios for its authentic aesthetic and quality.

Looking Forward

As entire studios continues to grow, Hunt and Diaz remain committed to their founding principles. Future plans include expanding their sustainable practices, introducing new seasonal collections, and exploring innovative fabrics—all while maintaining the accessibility that defines the brand.

"We're not just building a brand," Diaz reflects. "We're proving that the fashion industry can change. Luxury doesn't have to be exclusive. Quality doesn't have to be expensive. And great design should be for everyone."
