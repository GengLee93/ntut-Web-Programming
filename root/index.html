Personal Introduction

Nicole Ardoin is the Sykes Family Director of the Emmett Interdisciplinary Program in Environment and Resources, Associate Professor in the Graduate School of Education, and Senior Fellow in the Woods Institute for the Environment. Her research explores social-ecological systems, environmental education, and decision-making processes.

Research Interests

  • Civic Education
  • Collaborative Learning
  • Environmental Education
  • Gender Issues
  • Lifelong Learning
  • Sociology
